Our Mission

I (Andrea) started organizing events/workshops in 2000 and I did for at least 12 years, so I'm perfectly aware of the difficoulty of finding a proper place where to organize one. Workshop Venues aims to be a service where people can look for a venue, compare characteristics and prices, write reviews and submit new venues.

We want to provide also a rest API for anyone who wants to build an application based on our service or just to access our data. Venues data can be submitted by everyone who use our service, so we belive that it should be accessible by anyone as well.

Why we do it (really)

Other than trying to provide a useful service, our intent is to improve our Python/Django skills, experiment with technologies and libraries that can be useful for our everyday job and, last but not least, because we think it's funny to code together.
